About Us

PetKingdom PH was established on October 2, 2021, by a group of siblings and cousins who share a deep love for animals. Located at San Lorenzo Place Mall, 2F, the store serves as a one-stop shop for pet owners, offering a wide range of high-quality pet supplies along with pet grooming and pet boarding.

In addition to its physical store, PetKingdom PH is available online where customers can set grooming appointments via Facebook & Instagram and purchase supplies conveniently through Shopee. Committed to the well-being of pets and the convenience of their owners, PetKingdom PH continues to grow as a trusted destination for pet care and supplies.

Our Vision

To be the leading pet care destination in the Philippines, providing a complete and convenient experience for pet owners while ensuring the happiness, health, and well-being of all pets.

Our Mission

  • To offer a wide selection of premium pet supplies, ensuring pets have everything they need to thrive.
  • To provide professional and compassionate pet grooming services that keep pets healthy and well-groomed.
  • To create a safe and comfortable boarding experience for pets, giving pet owners peace of mind while they shop.
  • To foster a community of responsible pet ownership by sharing knowledge, expertise, and quality products.
